AP review control points

  • Confirm the email contains an invoice before routing work forward.
  • Search Zone to avoid duplicate upload or rework.
  • Compare the invoice PO to the PO dropdown before processing.
  • Confirm the invoice has a matching IR listed under the PO.
  • Route missing PO, mismatch, and missing IR exceptions to the right owner.
